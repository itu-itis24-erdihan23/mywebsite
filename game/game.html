Let's Play the Game!

How To Play

-Guess one letter at a time or the entire word.

-Each correct letter earns 20 points.

If the entire word is guessed correctly, you win the game.

-Players have 3 lives; one life is lost for each incorrect guess.

-The game is lost when lives run out.

-Press the "Reset Game" button to restart.
